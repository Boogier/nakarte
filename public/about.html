About Run-Balkan

This site is developed by Alexey Kozlov aka Boogier, a trail runner, to promote outdoor activities and give you ideas for exploration of the Balkan region. And not only the Balkans!

The database includes tracks for Serbia, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Montenegro, North Macedonia, Albania, Turkey, Georgia, Armenia and it is constantly growing.

Planning your weekend? Find interesting places to run and to hike. Download gpx tracks, see pictures along the routes.

Open Source

This site is open source software built on base of Nakarte. The source code is available on GitHub.

Third party

Strava is the initial storage of all the tracks. The landscape photos used by this site are stored in Strava CDN.
